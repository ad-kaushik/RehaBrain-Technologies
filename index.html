About Us

RehaBrain Technologies LLP is a neuro-rehabilitation startup focused on developing innovative solutions for upper-limb motor recovery. We integrate neuroscience, engineering, and emerging technologies such as virtual reality (VR) to create engaging, data-driven rehabilitation systems for assessment and therapy. Founded by engineers from IIT Delhi, RehaBrain aims to bridge the gap between clinical rehabilitation needs and advanced technological interventions, enabling more effective, accessible, and patient-centric neuro-rehabilitation.
